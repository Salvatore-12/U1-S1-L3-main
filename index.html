Focaccia with caramelized Onions,
Cranberries,and Brie

wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizier topped with sweet caramelized onions,tart
cranberries,and creamy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4cups(500g)all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1_2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired,thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

Make the focaccia dough

IN a medium-size mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment,mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorber.You don't need to actually knead the dough,though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorrporate the last of the flour...
